CUBA MOBILE BANKING

Digital banking for the modern age

Overview

Our client, a leading bank, wanted to create a mobile banking application that provides a seamless user experience, enabling customers to easily access and manage their accounts, make transactions, and receive personalized recommendations for financial products and services.

Role

UI/UX Designer

Year

2021

Software

Adobe XD

Goal

Our primary goal was to design an intuitive and user-friendly application that would enable customers to complete their banking tasks with ease.

Design Phase

Research

We conducted a series of user interviews and surveys to understand customers' needs and pain points when it comes to mobile banking. Our research revealed the following key insights:

  1. Customers want a simple and intuitive application that enables them to quickly and easily access their accounts and perform transactions.
  2. Security is a top concern for customers when it comes to mobile banking.
  3. Customers want personalized recommendations for financial products and an in-app marketplace.
Competitive Analysis

We conducted a competitive analysis of similar mobile banking apps to find out how they met the needs of their customers. We found that the most successful apps were those that provided a variety of features, such as account management, bill payments, and money transfers. These apps also had user-friendly interfaces that were easy to navigate and allowed users to access their accounts quickly.

Personas

Based on our research, we created a user persona for our target user, Martins and Ayarr. They both want a mobile banking app that is easy to use, secure, and convenient. They want an app that allows them to view their accounts, pay bills, transfer money, and apply for loans quickly and easily.

Site Map

We started by creating a site map to organize the content and structure of the app. The site map included pages for account management, bill payments, money transfers, loan applications, and other banking services.

Wireframes

Next, we created wireframes to visualize the layout of the app. We used a simple and clean design with a focus on usability. The wireframes included clear call-to-action buttons, easy navigation, and a consistent color scheme. Once the wireframes were finalized, we created high-fidelity mockups to showcase the final design of the app. The mockups included images and text that reflected the bank's branding and message.

Features and Functionality

The application includes the following features and functionality:

  1. Account summary: Customers can view a summary of their account balances and recent transactions.
  2. Transaction history: Customers can view a detailed transaction history for each of their accounts.
  3. Transfer money: Customers can transfer money between their accounts or to another account within the bank.
  4. Bill payments: Customers can pay their bills directly from the application.
  5. Personalized recommendations: Based on their financial history and behavior, customers can receive personalized recommendations for financial products and services.
  6. Security: The application includes a range of security features, including multi-factor authentication and biometric authentication (such as fingerprint or face recognition).
  7. In-app marketplace: Customers can purchase stocks, book flight tickets, and shop via the app with free delivery to any part of the continent.

Conclusion:

In conclusion, our team designed a mobile banking application that provides a seamless user experience, enabling customers to easily access and manage their accounts, make transactions, and receive personalized recommendations for financial products and services. Our design process focused on ease of use, security, and personalization, and we conducted user testing to ensure that the application meets customers' needs and expectations. We believe that this application will enable our client to provide a better banking experience for their customers, and we look forward to seeing the positive impact it will have on their business.
