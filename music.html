Research

I conducted a series of user interviews and surveys to understand users' needs and pain points when it comes to music streaming applications.

Interview Goals

  • I want to understand common challenges people face streaming their favorite music
  • I want to identify frustrations people experience during the process of streaming

    • Interview Questions

  • Can you describe your current schedule and how you balance your responsibilities with streaming on your favorite music app?
  • How often do you use the music app? When you do, what is your motivation for doing so?
  • What challenges do you face using the music app? How does this make you feel?
  • Is there anyway in which you feel the challenges could be resolved?

    • Research Insights

    My research revealed the following key insights:

  • Users want an application that is easy to navigate and search for music.
  • Users want personalized recommendations for new music based on their listening history.
  • Users want to be able to create and share playlists with friends.

    Problem Statement

    Nina is a student who needs a effective music app with curated playlists because she wants to listen to her favorite playlists and share with friends.

    Hypothesis Statement

    Nina needs a music app that provides personalized listening experience, create playlists and share her favorite songs with friends.

    Conclusion: In conclusion, I designed a music streaming application that provides an immersive and personalized listening experience, enabling users to easily discover new music, create playlists, and share their favorite songs with friends. My design process focused on ease of use, personalization, and social sharing, and I conducted user testing to ensure that the application meets users' needs and expectations. I believe that this application will provide a better music streaming experience for it's users.

    Features and Functionality:

    The application includes the following features and functionality:

    1. Home screen: Users can view personalized music recommendations based on their listening history and preferences.
    2. Search: Users can search for specific songs, artists, or albums.
    3. Playlists: Users can create and save their own playlists or browse and follow public playlists created by other users.
    4. Sharing: Users can share songs, playlists, and artists with friends on social media or via email.
    5. Radio: Users can listen to radio stations based on specific genres or artists.
    6. Premium subscription: Users can sign up for a premium subscription to access ad-free listening, offline playback, and other premium features.
