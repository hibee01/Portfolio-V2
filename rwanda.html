Project Overview

Rwandaful app is a platform that highlights the beauty of Rwanda, from destinations, events, things to do, people, and cultures.

Problem

Rwandaful's current app is confusing and overwhelming for users, causing low user engagement and a high bounce rate. The app is difficult to navigate and does not provide enough relevant information to help users make informed decisions when planning their trips.

Research

We started by conducting user research to understand the pain points of the current app and what users are looking for in a travel app. We conducted user interviews and analyzed data from app analytics to identify common user issues. Our research showed that users want a travel app that is easy to navigate, provides relevant and accurate information, and includes social features that help users connect with other travelers. Our research revealed the following key insights:

  1.  Lack of comprehensive information about destinations, events, accommodations & things to do
  2.  Difficulty finding travel agencies
  3.  Lack of budget ideas & planning for a vacation
  4.  Not enough local information about the people & their cultures
  5.  Expensive travel packages online
  6.  Fraudulent agencies online

Strategy

We developed a strategy that focuses on simplifying the app's navigation and providing users with relevant and accurate information.

Design

We began the design process by creating wireframes that reflect the strategy. We focused on creating an app that is easy to navigate and provides users with relevant information. The app is divided into four sections:

  1. Home Screen: The home screen includes a search bar where users can enter their desired destination and travel dates. It also includes a section for featured destinations and travel deals.
  2. Destination Screen: The destination screen provides users with relevant information about the destination, including popular attractions, restaurants, and hotels. It also includes a map that shows the location of each attraction and the ability to book tours and activities.
  3. Booking Screen: The booking screen allows users to book flights, hotels, and rental cars.

Final Product

After testing and iterating on the design, the final app features a simple and easy-to-navigate design that provides users with relevant and accurate information. The home screen provides a clear starting point for users to plan their trips, while the destination screen provides detailed information about the destination. The booking screen allows users to book their travel arrangements with ease.

Results

After launching the new app, Rwandaful saw a significant increase in user engagement and a decrease in bounce rates. Users spent more time on the app and made more bookings than before. The new app helped Rwandaful reach its goals of improving user experience and increasing conversions.
