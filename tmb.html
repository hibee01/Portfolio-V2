Tapmybio

A single link in your Instagram or Twitter to guide users to multiple destinations.

Overview

Tmb allows users create personalized and customizable page that houses all their important links that they want to share with their audiences. It's a link-in-bio solution that can be used on social platforms like Instagram, TikTok, Twitch, Facebook, YouTube, Twitter, LinkedIn, and more. It aids influencers, celebrities, gamers etc in the discovery of their work, brand, or business.

Problem

Tapmybio current landing page is cluttered and difficult to navigate, causing high bounce rates and low user engagement. They are looking to redesign their landing page to improve user experience and increase conversions.

Problem

Tapmybio current landing page is cluttered and difficult to navigate, causing high bounce rates and low user engagement. They are looking to redesign their landing page to improve user experience and increase conversions.

Research

We started by conducting user research to understand the pain points of their current landing page and what users are looking for in a social media link aggregator website. We conducted user interviews and analyzed data from website analytics to identify common user issues. Our research showed that users want a clean and easy-to-use website with clear calls-to-action and customization options. Users also expressed a desire for more features to improve the customization of their tmb.

Strategy

We developed a strategy that focuses on simplifying the landing page and highlighting the customization options available to users. We will also include information on the benefits of using Tmb.

Design

We began the design process by creating wireframes that reflect the strategy. We focused on creating a clean and easy-to-use landing page that showcases the benefits of using Tmb and highlights the customization options available to users. The landing page is divided into two major sections:

  1. Hero Section: The hero section features a large banner image that showcases the benefits of using Tmb. It includes a headline, a subheadline, and a call-to-action (CTA) button.
  2. Features Section: The features section includes a list of the various features that Tmb offers, including integration with social media profiles and analytics. This section helps users understand the full capabilities of Tmb and its value.

Final Product

After testing and iterating on the design, the final landing page features a simple and clean design that focuses on the benefits of using Tmb and highlights the customization options available to users. The hero section provides a clear call-to-action and sets the tone for the rest of the page. The customization section provides a step-by-step guide that helps users create a custom link tree, while the features section highlights the various features of Tmb and its value.
