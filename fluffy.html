Frenchie

Designing a pet landing page.

Background:

A local pet store has recently launched a new pet store and wants to increase awareness and sales through their website. They need a landing page that effectively communicates the value of their new products and drives users to make a purchase.

Goals:

  1. Increase awareness and sales of the new pet food products
  2. Create a user-friendly and visually appealing landing page
  3. Clearly communicate the benefits of the new products

Role

UI/UX Designer

Year

2022

Software

Figma

Challenges:

  1. Ensuring that the landing page is informative without being overwhelming or cluttered.
  2. Ensuring that the landing page is visually appealing and engaging for users.
  3. Creating a clear and concise message that effectively communicates the value of the new products.

Research:

To inform the design of the landing page, we conducted user research to better understand the needs and preferences of potential customers. We interviewed pet owners and conducted a survey to gather insights on their shopping habits, product preferences, and pain points.

Design:

With these insights in mind, we designed a landing page that is visually appealing and easy to navigate. The landing page features a hero image of a happy pet owner, which immediately captures users' attention and creates a positive emotional response. The messaging is clear and concise, highlighting the key benefits of the new products. To encourage users to make a purchase, we included a steps to purchase and a prominent "Buy Now" button that leads users to a simple checkout process. The checkout process is straightforward, with clear instructions and visual cues to guide users through the process.
